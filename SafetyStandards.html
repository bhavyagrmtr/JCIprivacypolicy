Safety Standards Against CSAE

Last Updated: May 31, 2025

At JCI Zone 2, we are committed to creating a safe, secure, and respectful environment for all users. We strictly prohibit any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation on our platform and take proactive measures to detect, report, and prevent such behavior.

Our Commitments:
  • Zero Tolerance Policy: We do not allow content or behavior that exploits or endangers minors in any way.
  • Moderation & Reporting: All user-generated content and profiles are monitored. Suspicious activity can be reported by users and is reviewed by admins.
  • Verification: New user registrations require mobile OTP verification and admin approval before being activated.
  • Secure Data Handling: We protect user information and do not share or expose sensitive data.
  • Collaboration with Authorities: We cooperate with law enforcement if required and report any CSAE-related violations immediately.
How to Report a Concern:

If you come across any suspicious activity or content involving children, please contact us immediately at:
Email: support@jcizone2.org

We are committed to maintaining the safety of all our users, especially minors, and will take all necessary actions against any violations.
