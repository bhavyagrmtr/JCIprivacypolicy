Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how our application collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information Collection

When you register on our app, we collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Date of Birth
  • Occupation
  • LO Name (Local Organization)
  • Profile Picture

2. Use of Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Verify your identity through OTP
  • Display user profiles in the app upon admin approval
  • Allow admin to manage and approve user access
  • Enable community visibility in a secure and respectful environment

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. Only approved users and admins can view profiles within the app.

4. Data Security

We use secure protocols and industry best practices to protect your data. Profile edits require admin approval for sensitive fields like name and mobile number.

5. User Rights

You may request data correction via admin. If you want your account removed, contact the admin through the app.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy. Users will be notified of any changes via in-app notification.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact the administrator through the app portal.
